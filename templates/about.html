About AI Health Mate

AI Health Mate is your personal AI-powered health assistant, designed to help you take charge of your health. Our advanced algorithms analyze your health data to assess risks for multiple diseases and guide you towards informed medical decisions.

From predicting heart disease to screening for infections like malaria or typhoid, we make healthcare more accessible, understandable, and proactive.

How It Works

1. Enter Your Data

Provide key health metrics from your latest medical reports or tests.

2. AI Analysis

Our system evaluates your inputs using medically informed risk models.

3. Get Recommendations

Receive a risk level and guidance on whether to consult a doctor.
